Play like Pujara and Rahane: Australian batters receive advice after MCG debacle,Former India cricketer Robin Uthappa has urged Australian batters to adopt patience like Cheteshwar Pujara and Ajinkya Rahane on challenging wickets. His advice comes after Australia's heavy collapse during the fourth Test of the Ashes 2025-26 in Melbourne.
